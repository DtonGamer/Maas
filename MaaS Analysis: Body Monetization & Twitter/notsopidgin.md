## Video 6: "Content Batching: Work Smarter, Not Harder" (60 seconds)

**Hook (0-3s):** "Stop creating content daily—there's a much more efficient system"

**Body (3-50s):**
"Professional creators work in batches, not through daily chaos:

**The Batch Production Method**:
- Day 1: Shoot 20-40 content pieces in one 2-4 hour session (when NEPA brings light)
- Day 2: Edit everything in one focused session
- Weeks 2-3: Schedule and post consistently (even when power goes off)

Why this method works brilliantly for Nigerian creators:
- **Electricity problems solved**: Create when power is available, your schedule isn't held hostage by NEPA
- **Data efficiency**: Upload everything once with strong wifi connection, no daily data waste
- **Guaranteed consistency**: You won't miss posting even during your worst days
- **Higher quality**: Dedicated production time yields better content
- **Mental peace**: Content creation doesn't consume your entire life

Let's compare the numbers:
- Daily method: 1 hour per day (setup + shoot + edit) = 30 hours monthly, constant data usage
- Batch method: 4 hours shooting + 4 hours editing = 8 hours monthly, single upload session

That's 22 hours saved monthly that you can invest in marketing, customer service, and actually collecting your money. Plus you've saved on data costs wey don increase 50% for 2025.

**Pro strategy**: Shoot when electricity is stable, schedule posts using free wifi at restaurants or co-working spaces."
