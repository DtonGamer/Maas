The **Discovery Call** (also called the qualification or explanation call) is the most critical step in your recruitment pipeline. This is where you transition from vague interest to full, informed commitment by clearly explaining the adult content business while testing genuine interest and boundaries.

It's typically a **15–30 minute** video call (Zoom, WhatsApp, Telegram, Google Meet, etc.) — video is strongly preferred because it builds trust, lets you read body language, and confirms the person is real/engaged.
