## The Cryptocurrency Implication: Forced Compliance or Exclusion

Now let's address what the ISO document reveals about cryptocurrencies. The highlighted section states explicitly: "There is a lot of confusion and misleading information on the web referring to ISO 20022 compliant cryptocurrencies but those statements are not correct. This is the banking organ drawing a clear boundary line. ISO 20022 is a standard for financial institutions exchanging payment information through established networks like SWIFT. Cryptocurrencies, by their design, operate outside this institutional framework. They have their own protocols—Bitcoin uses its blockchain, Ethereum uses its blockchain, and so on.
